Careers

You could be the next "Pawn Star"!

We are a family owned pawn shop chain with multiple locations and now have openings for full and part time employees at our Baton Rouge store.

At Pelican Pawn, we are always looking for top talent. We are Louisiana's largest pawn shop with locations in Baton Rouge, Denham Springs, Gonzales, and Jefferson. At Pelican Pawn we strive to make a fast paced, fun environment for our employees to work in. A pawn broker is a very special type of person with unique abilities and we are looking for those special people.

They must be aware of what's going on around them at all times, have empathy for the customers, be able to multitask, and not be prone to mistakes. they must have computer knowledge, be bondable and in goo physical condition - able to lift TVs etc. when necessary.

A successful applicant will have the following skills/attributes:

  • Entrepreneurial spirit
  • Desire to never stop learning
  • Problem solving ability
  • Compassion for the public
  • Fast thinking
  • Negotiation skills
  • Excellent verbal communication skills
  • Computer savvy

Preferred Experience in one or more of the following job roles:

  • Loan Officer
  • Jewelry Sales
  • Gun Sales
  • Small Engine Repair
  • Gun Sales
  • Car Audio
  • Car Sales
  • Management Experience
  • Pawnbroker Experience
  • Jeweler
  • Gunsmith
  • Gold, Sliver or Diamond Buyer
  • Computer Repair
  • Musical Instrument Sales and Repair
  • Tool Dealer
  • Furniture Sales
  • Service Industry

Our team members must be available for shifts Monday - Friday 9:30 - 6:00 and Saturday

Benefits Include:

  • No work on nights or Sundays
  • Group insurance
  • Paid vacation
  • Lucrative bonus system
  • 401k for full time employees
  • Great working environment

We pay $9-14 per hour depending on skill and experience

If you have previous pawn shop experience or feel like you have what it takes to be a pawnbroker, please stop by and fill out an application or send in your resume to: pelicanpawn@yahoo.com
